262 Piano Class IV (0.25)

This continuation of 261 emphasizes sight playing, score reading, harmonization and transposition, more advanced improvisation and harmonic usage, performance preparation, and leadership from the keyboard. A grade of B- or higher satisfies Piano Proficiency Level 4 requirements. Prerequisite: Music 261, completion of Piano Proficiency 3, or audition placement. Recommended co-requisites: Music 214. Offered annually in the spring semester.
