Performance Studies: Principal

 

Full credit lessons every semester once admitted to the program (minimum of 6)

 

1 semester collaborative keyboard performance studies (1.0) (in addition to the 6 minimum semesters full credit solo lessons)
Performance Studies: Secondary

4 semesters on 1 instrument. Up to 2 semesters of secondary voice may be taken in voice class (164). Up to 4 semesters of secondary piano may be taken in piano classes (161, 162, 261, 262).

Keyboard Proficiency Level IV
Performance (principal instrucment/voice)

• 2 solo performances on official student recitals
• 30 minute Junior recital (or, by petition only: 3 additional solo performances on official student recitals)
• 60-minute Senior recital

Collaborative Keyboard
In addition:
• 2 collaborative performances on official student recitals, one must be in junior or senior year
Official Ensemble Participation Every semester once admitted to BM performance
Concert Attendance (see music handbook) Documented attendance at 60 concerts
